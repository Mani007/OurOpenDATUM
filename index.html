The Hidden Pandemic

Plastics Waste

This plastic waste get degrade into micro and nano size particles, also known as Micro-plastics.

Micro-Plastics and Environment

These Micro-plastics will mix in the Environment and will pollute it that no instrument can measure accurately.

Micro-Plastics and Humans

Reaseach has shown that humans consume more than 70000 Micro-plastics particle every year.

Artificial Intelligence for Micro-Plastics degradation and Toxicology

Our OpenDATUM- Open Sorce tool, with Artificial Intelligence, can be able to predict Micro-plastics degradation and Toxicology across different parts of the world.

Project Road-Map

  1. March 2022

    Inception of Idea

    Leveraging power of Artificial intelligence in prediction of degradation of microplastics.

  2. May 2022

    Proof of concept

    Making data preprocessing and trying out different time series analysis and prediction algorithms such as ARIMA, SARIMA etc.

  3. June 2022

    OpenDATUM Origin

    Creating OpenDatum, the platform to accelerate the research of microplatic degradation and toxicology.

  4. June 2023

    OpenDatum

    Platform BETA launch with minium important features. Also include our AI models.

  5. September 2023

    OpenDatum

    Developers API and documentations released under MIT license

  6. November 2023

    OpenDATUM Globally

    The suite of tools to be launched in the global scale with realtime data processing and inference capablity.

  7. December 2023

    OpenDatum on Blockchains

    Complete side chain solution for multiple leading blockchains such as Ethereum, Polygon etc.
